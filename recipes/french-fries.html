French Fries Recipe

Description

Homemade crispy french fries seasoned with salt.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into evenly sized fries.
  2. Rinse the cut potatoes under cold water to remove excess starch.
  3. Place the potatoes in a large bowl of cold water and let them soak for 30 minutes.
  4. Drain the potatoes and pat them dry with a kitchen towel.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 325째F (160째C).
  6. Carefully add the potatoes to the hot oil and fry them for about 5-6 minutes or until they are pale golden and slightly soft.
  7. Remove the partially cooked fries from the oil and let them drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
  8. Increase the oil temperature to 375째F (190째C).
  9. Return the partially cooked fries to the hot oil and fry them until they are golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes.
  10. Remove the fries from the oil, season with salt, and serve hot.

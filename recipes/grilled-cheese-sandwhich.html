Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Description

A classic grilled cheese sandwich made with melted cheese and crispy bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your favorite)

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium heat.

    2. Step 2

  2. Butter one side of each bread slice.

    3. Step 3

  3. Place one slice, buttered side down, in the skillet.

    4. Step 4

  4. Place the cheese slices on top of the bread in the skillet.

    5. Step 5

  5. Place the second bread slice on top, buttered side up.

    6. Step 6

  6. Cook until the bottom bread slice is golden brown, then flip the sandwich.

    7. Step 7

  7. Cook until the second side is golden brown and the cheese is melted.

    8. Step 8

  8. Remove from the skillet, cut in half if desired, and serve hot.
